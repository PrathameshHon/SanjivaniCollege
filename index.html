About Computer Engineering @ Sanjivani

A strong foundation in programming, problem solving and modern technologies.

Department Overview

The Computer Engineering Department focuses on building your fundamentals in programming, data structures, mathematics, digital electronics and communication skills. From first year itself, students are encouraged to explore coding, projects and innovation.

  • Outcome based teaching–learning
  • Modern laboratories and computing facilities
  • Workshops, seminars and expert talks
  • Active student associations and clubs

First Year Focus Areas

In the first year, you will mainly learn the basics that are common to all branches as well as introductory subjects related to computers.

  • Engineering Mathematics & Physics
  • Fundamentals of Programming (C / Python etc.)
  • Basic Electrical / Electronics & Engineering Graphics
  • Communication skills & professional ethics
